Step 2: Filter Extraction

Please ensure the water inlet is closed before proceeding.

A

Locate the Stage 3 Blue Housing in the center of the manifold assembly. Use the provided filter wrench if it is hand-tight.

B

Twist the housing counter-clockwise (approximately 1/4 turn) until it clicks, then pull downwards to release.

warning

Minor water spillage is normal during extraction. Keep a cloth handy to wipe down the base of the manifold.
